My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Avengers: Endgame or Avengers: Infinity War

I have this as a tie with it's prior movie, "Avengers: Infinity War". These movies were both a long time wait for it's fans and they did not dissapoint. It had everything for comdedy, spectacular action, and even hit me in the feels.

Spider-Man: Into the Spider-Verse

This is probably one of my favorite animated movies of all time. It was nice to see another Spider-Man other tha the Peter Parker we all know. From the story telling to awesome art-style. 10 out of 10

Creed III

I really enjoyed this third installment in the Credd franchise. Not only is it a great and well choreographed boxing film, it had a really nice story. The story of two brother-like characters who have a falling out. And the little anime references were a nice touch.
